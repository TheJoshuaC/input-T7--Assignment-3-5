Group Processes

Input T7 worked well together on assignment 2. As a group, we were able to effectively communicate with each other and delegate tasks based on our individual strengths. We had a weekly video call to discuss progress, assign tasks to each other and give each other feedback on our work. When it came to any obstacle, we were able to discuss the problems as a group and decide our next course of action. This was assisted using a google form which would give all team members the opportunity to vote on what they thought would be the best choice to make.

In the upcoming Assignment 3, there is room for improvement. To work better on the upcoming assignment, we recognized that we need to establish clear goals and expectations for all team members from the beginning of the project. For assignment 3, we created a project plan and delegated tasks to each team member, with more stringent deadlines. Another way in which we can improve is by setting aside time near the end of the project, for us to go over the entire document, and make sure that it is concise throughout.
