Skills & Jobs

Out of the many professions in the IT industry we looked at, the four that stand out as important to ensure the success for this project would be a UI artist/designer, Programmer, Security Consultant, and a Full Stack Developer.

For the project we are working on, the product would be accessible through a webservice. For Dungeon Dice to be as user friendly as possible, we need to have the webpage be appealing, thus the need the need for a UI artist/designer. The skills they provide to the team are crucial as the user experience matters quite a lot for retention of users. They would need to work closely with the programmer to make sure the result works well with the vision of the entire project team.

The expertise of a professional UI artist will have the knowledge and experience of previous projects, this will provide a valuable point of perspective that we can use to increase the engagement in the future.

A Programmer is necessary in every situation as they are the bread and butter of an IT project. They are the key in transforming a project idea into a working project. Through their expertise in programming languages, they can use the best fit for our project goal to deliver a well thought out and functional project. The programmer would have to be adaptable to the needs of the project, preferably having experience with an agile methodology used in prior jobs.

A Security Consultant is an integral part of the team as they can use their knowledge to protect not only the team/company's data but also the data of the users of the application. The project may rely on user data, such as log in information and that would pose a security risk that the consultant would be able to apply their expertise to prevent the theft of data from hackers and breaches.

A Full Stack Developer would be a valuable member of the project creation process as they are the ones who will focus on integrating the Front-end and Back-end, ensuring that all parts of the project come together.

Their knowledge of databases and programming languages will provide the tracks all sectors of the project will require to make this project possible. A Full Stack Developer needs to be able to communicate with everyone involved in the project so that the result is what the team had envisioned.

Working closely with the Programmer and the Security Consultant so they can build a well-designed as well as secure database for both the project and the future users of Dungeon Dice.
